My Personal Code

Justice Before Judgment: I will seek truth before issuing blame. If punishment must fall, it will fall with purpose‚Äînot to show strength, but to uphold peace.

Compassion Is Not Weakness: I will not mistake cruelty for discipline. The weak deserve protection, not condemnation.

The Spirit, Not Just the Words: I will know the Measure, respect it, and live by its heart‚Äîeven when its wording falls short of wisdom.

Mercy Is a Blade of Its Own: I will spare those who yield, offer redemption where it is sought, and remember that every foe was once a child.

I Do Not Serve a Throne‚ÄîI Serve the People: My loyalty is to those who suffer, not those who rule. If their will diverges, I will side with the helpless.

I Will Lead When Needed, Follow When Right, and Stand Alone When Necessary: Honor means choosing the hard path when it is the right one, even if I walk it alone.

My Knightly Vow

I am Darrian Rendorie, and I swear this oath before the gods of good, the Order of the Sword, and the memory of those who have fallen in defense of honor.
I vow to stand between the innocent and the shadow, to wield my blade not for glory but for justice.
I will act with courage, tempered by compassion.
I will hold truth above comfort, and mercy above pride.
I will serve the spirit of the Measure, not just its letters‚Äîfor law without wisdom is tyranny, and obedience without thought is cowardice.
I will fight with faith, but I will never let faith blind me to suffering.
Let my sword be swift against evil, my shield steadfast for the helpless, and my voice unshaken in the presence of fear.
This I swear, in honor of my mentor, in memory of the fallen, and in service to all who cannot lift a blade of their own.

Words for the Path

General Purpose

A Knight of Solamnia does not ask if we should do the right thing‚Äîonly how.

I serve not only my blade, but the souls it must protect.

Your quarrel is not with me. It is with the ideals you have abandoned.

Honor is the armor no blade can pierce.

We are the wall against the coming night. Let us not be found crumbling.

Morning Prayer

Let my blade be swift, my heart be still, and my cause remain just. The day is new, and so must I be.

I rise not for glory, but for duty. Let the light find no shadow in my soul.

Another dawn, another chance to stand between the world and the dark. Let me be worthy of the space I hold.

The sun rises, and with it, my duty. May I meet it without faltering.

Evening Reflection

May my sword sleep clean, my shield be heavy with mercy, and my choices sit quiet in my chest.

If I erred today, let the gods forgive me. If I stood true, let the weary rest easy.

The Measure may judge the day, but I must judge my heart. And tonight, it is heavy‚Äîbut not ashamed.

The day is done. The deeds are weighed. May my conscience be my softest pillow.

A Tough Moral Choice

The law may speak, but it is not the voice I listen for. I listen for the cry of the one it forgets.

What is right is not always what is written. And what is written is not always right.

I do not fear consequences. I fear failing to be the man Isben believed I could become.

Sometimes, the harder path is the one that leads home.

A thousand rules cannot make a single right choice.

Before Battle

The blade is not what makes me a knight. What I fight for does.

Stand tall, strike true. And if this is the end, let it be the kind they tell stories about.

My shield is not for myself. It is for the one who cannot raise theirs.

Let the world know we did not kneel to fear.

Let our courage be a light so bright it blinds our foes.

Defying Unjust Authority

I serve Solamnia‚Äîbut not the hand that strangles her.

The Measure does not protect tyrants‚Äîit protects those they would trample.

When Offering Mercy

Yield, and I will show you the mercy I wish had been shown to others.

I take no pride in bloodshed. Lay down your blade and walk away with your soul.

I do not want your life. I want you to make something better of it.

The truest victory is not in crushing a foe, but in helping them rise from their own defeat.

Even the smallest candle pushes back the dark. I will be that candle.

My path may seem strange, but it leads to the same dawn we all seek.
